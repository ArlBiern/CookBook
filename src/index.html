Culinary curosity

Instructions

+

Enter the searched ingredients in the input field. During write you should get some hints. Select the ingredient from list and press "Add" button. There are three categories of meals: main course, dessert, cocktail. You should choose three ingredients for the main course and one for dessert and cocktail.

After pressing the button a card with the ingredient will appear. Grab it and drop it on the pot.

The ingredient should appear in the table under the pot in the appropriate column. You can delete any ingredient with "x" or clean the entire table with the "clear table" button.

Now click the "search" button, which will take you to the meal suggestions with the ingredients of your choice.
